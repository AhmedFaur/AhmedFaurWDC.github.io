What is Electricity

It is the transfer of a group of electrons from one point to another

What is Electric Current

Electric current, in simple terms, is a stream of electrons that flows through a conductor

Conductive and Insulating Materials Conductors:

Materials that have a large number of free electrons that can move from one atom to another to create an electric current like: Silver, Gold, Aluminium, Copper

Insulating materials:

They are materials that prevent their electrons from transferring and thus prevent the presence of any electrical current
